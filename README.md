The Economic Impact of Colombian Presidential Tweets on Financial
Markets
================

# Project Overview

This project analyzes the economic impact of tweets posted by the
President of Colombia on key financial market indicators.

The objective is to evaluate whether presidential communication via
Twitter generates measurable effects on:

- Exchange rate (TRM)
- Financial market indices
- Investor sentiment
- Market volatility

The analysis combines text mining techniques with time series methods to
assess short-term and dynamic effects.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Research Question

Do presidential tweets produce statistically significant movements in
Colombian financial markets?

Sub-questions:

- Does tweet sentiment correlate with TRM movements?
- Are certain policy-related tweets (e.g., labor, pension, health
  reform) associated with abnormal returns?
- Is there evidence of anticipatory or lagged market reactions?

------------------------------------------------------------------------
